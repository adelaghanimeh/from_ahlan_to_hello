📘 Grammar Fun: Talking About the Future!

🌟 What does "will" mean?

We use will when we talk about something that is going to happen in the future — not now, but later!

Today I’m reading a book.

Tomorrow, I will go to the library!

🔤 The Magic Formula:

I / You / He / She / We / They + will + verb

✏️ Examples:

I will eat ice cream. 🍦

She will play outside. 🏃‍♀️

They will learn a new language. 📚

🧠 Remember:

  • Will = shows the future
  • Learn / play / eat = action words (verbs)
